Creating an account:

To create an account simply click the Login/Signup button at the top of the webpage. Then click the sign up button which is below the heading Don't have an account create one here. Simply enter your information and click create account. User names and email addresses are limited to 45 characters, passwords are limited to 64 characters.


Logging in:

To log in click the Login/SignUp button at the top of the webpage and then enter your email address and password below the heading sign in and click the login button. Once logged in you will be able to edit your account information and view and or cancel your bookings.


Changing your account infomation:

To change your account information you first need to be logged into your account. Click the account button at the top of the webpage. Then select what you want to edit e.g(Your user name, email address, password) Then click the edit information button. You will be prompted to enter your new information twice, once entered click the confirm button, your account information is now updated.


Logging out:

To log out of your account click the logout button at the top of the webpage.


Deleting your account

To delete your account you need to be logged in first. Click the account button at the top of the webpage. Then click the delete account button. Enter your password twice and click delete account. Your account will then be deleted


Making a booking:

To start the booking process you do not need to be logged in. To start a booking enter your desired origin and destination, look at the time table by clicking show time table at the top of the page for valid journeys you can book. select what class you want to fly default is Economy, and input your date of travel. Once filled click the get prices button.

Once clicked you will see a list of available flights from your chosen origin and destination on the date you selected. Prices will vary depending on how far in advance you select and what flight you choose. Select the booking depending on what the time to leave or arrive suits you. Click the continue booking button

If you are already logged in you will see the payment screen simply input your card information and click pay for booking ,you have now made a booking.

If you are not logged in you will be redirected to the login page where you can either log in or sign up, if you login you will then encounter the payment page. Follow the instructions above

Please make sure that your not accidently booking the wrong flight as there is no way to update bookings when booked, if you do book the wrong booking you will need to cancel the incorrect booking and rebook the correct flight. Refer to "Canceling a booking" for more infomation


Viewing a booking:

To do this you need to be logged in first. Click the bookings button at the top of the webpage. You will then be able to see all of the bookings you have made. To see the qr code unique for each booking click the view qr code button on the desired booking. You can then save the QR code for future use by right clicking and selecting save image.


Canceling a booking:

To cancel a booking you need to be logged in first. Click the bookings button at the top of the webpage. Then click the cancel booking button. Select the booking you would like to cancel and click delete booking. Depending on how far in advance you cancle you will need to pay a cancellation fee enter you card info and click the pay cancellation fee button. You have now cancelled your booking. Please make sure the booking you choose to cancel is the right booking as there is no way to refund / retrieve your booking after it is deleted.
